About Me

Hello! My name is Alessandro and I'm fourth-year and graduating senior at Northeastern University studying Finance. My academic journey has been focused on understanding how financial markets work and the origination of lucrative instruments. My minor in Data Science is supplementary, as it enables the analysis of vast amounts of market data, identifies patterns and trends, and enhances decision-making through predictive modeling and risk assessment.

Throughout my studies, I've developed skills in financial modeling, R, and JavaScript, which I use to analyze market data and create visualizations that enhance trading strategies and risk assessment. I believe that in sales and trading, effective data interpretation is not just about presenting numbers but about uncovering market trends, optimizing trade execution, and making informed decisions in fast-moving financial environments. I believe that effective data visualization is not just about presenting numbers, but about telling a story that can influence decision-making and drive action.

When I'm not working or studying, I enjoy soccer, cooking, and exploring the dining scene in Boston. I find that these activities help me maintain a balanced perspective and often inspire creative approaches to technical problems.

My goal is to leverage my analytical and technical skills to contribute to high-impact projects in sales and trading or private credit, where data-driven insights can improve deal structuring, pricing strategies, and portfolio risk management. I'm excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in the dynamic and evolving world of finance.
